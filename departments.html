What We Do

Department Overview

Core Printing

Variable Data Printing

India's largest colour variable data printing facility — each page printed uniquely with variable colours and data on the fly using our Miyakoshi MJP 600 and GravitasX presses.

  • 9 lakh+ impressions per day — full colour & grayscale
  • Simplex & Duplex modes — 4 Colour Front + 4 Colour Back
  • First Miyakoshi digital inkjet press in South East Asia
  • Banking statements, mutual fund statements, loyalty programs, telecom bills & more
Miyakoshi MJP 600 GravitasX
Finishing

Post-Press & Inserting

Fully automatic post-press equipment enabling quality work at faster speed — the inserter is the fastest in its category with an operational speed of 10,000 inserts per hour.

  • 2 Mueller Apparatebau gmbh sheeters for high-speed cutting
  • Automatic collating, folding & inserting with selective mail inserts
  • 100% automation — zero error printing & insertion
2 Muller Inserters 10,000 inserts/hr
Manufacturing

Envelope Manufacturing

High-speed machine-made envelopes using a W&D Mueller Apparatebau gmbh envelope machine — capable of producing multi-colour, multi-size envelopes at industrial scale.

  • 150,000 envelopes per day production capacity
  • Multi-colour & multi-size envelope manufacturing
  • First in India — High Speed Machine Made Envelopes
W&D Mueller Apparatebau gmbh Machine 150K/day
Dispatch

Mailing & Franking

Registered Business Postal Service Provider — dispatching statements economically and swiftly to all corners of India with an on-site post office authorised by India Post.

  • 2 Pitney Bowes franking machines — 200,000 per day
  • On-site post office — authorised by India Post
  • Envelopes segregated per destination, then dispatched via GPO pan-India
  • 1 Inland Letter Machine — 200,000/day capacity
India Post Authorised Pitney Bowes

  • Own secure site for data transfer

  • Personalisation with full colour graphics & one-to-one messaging
  • Zero-error printing — 100% accuracy via automation
