Who We Are

About Us

Kiran Business Forms Print Pvt Ltd, incorporated in 1993, is a printing and packaging company involved in variable data printing, automated mailroom facility, and flexible packaging. We are committed to sustainability with solar-powered operations, making us an eco-conscious packaging partner.

Spread across 12,000+ sq. ft. in the industrial area of Navi Mumbai, we are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company. We manufacture single and multi-layer laminates for a wide range of FMCG applications — Biscuit, Chips, Masala, Spices, Atta, Confectionery and more.
